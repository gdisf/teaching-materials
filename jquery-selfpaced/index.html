jQuery (Self-paced)

A self-paced workshop to introduce students to making webpages interactive with jQuery.


We will begin with introductions, a rundown of this workshop structure, and an introduction to jQuery.

After that, you'll work independently through the course until you reach a project. The course materials include videos, challenges, and quizzes. Please use your headphones for all videos.

When you reach a "Project," you'll skip it and work on it later with a pair. We'll give an intro to pair programming before the first project.

If you get stuck at any point, find a TA or post in the help chat room. We're here to help!
