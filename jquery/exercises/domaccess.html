jQuery: Exercise 2

Practicing DOM Access in jQuery

  • Open up girldevelopit.com in Chrome or Firefox, and open up the console.
  • Use jQuery to find the following parts of the page:
    • Every image on the page
    • The upper left-hand logo (that says GirlDevelopIt)
    • The logos of the media sources that featured GirlDevelopIt (lifeHacker, TED, etc., at the bottom of the page)
    • The big tagline in the middle (that says "Empowering women...")
    • All of the green colored city names (Baltimore, Buffalo, etc.)
    • The "CLASSES" text in the center area (underneath the Computer icon)
    • The donate link (in the top right menu)


$("img");
$(".logo");
$(".media");
$(".tagline")
$("a.green");
$(".blue .topic");
$("#donate a");
