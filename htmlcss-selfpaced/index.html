HTML & CSS (Self-paced)

A self-paced, beginner-level workshop designed to introduce students to making webpages with HTML and CSS.


Class materials and schedule

We will begin with brief lectures on how the web works and how to use Khan Academy.

After that, you'll work independently through the course. The course materials include videos, challenges, and quizzes. Please use your headphones for all videos.

When you reach a "Project", you'll skip it, and work on it later with a pair.

If you get stuck at any point, find a TA or post in the help chat room. We're here to help!


Intro Materials

  • Watch together: What is the web? and HTML basics
  • Complete together: Write a poem
  • Pair programming intro

Videos and Challenges

All videos and challenges are available here.

You should watch videos and complete challenges and exercises independently.


Pair Projects

A number of exercises in the course are labeled "Project". You will work on those with a pair during designated pairing time, so skip them when you see them and keep learning. We'll give an intro to pair programming before the first project.
